What's this?

It's about "image change"

Assuming you grant the page the acces to your camera, you should see three images.
One live feed of your camera, and two still images; the second being the previous one.
On the right you see three different "Difference Calculation Images".

Technical background: It's a simple "diff" of the RGB values of Still vs Previous. "Abs" are total accumulated diffs. "Per Pixel" is the average taking image dimensions into account
